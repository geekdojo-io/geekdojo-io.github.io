 Bitwise Operators

          

            

              
                

                  Operator
                  Explanation
                

              
              
                

                  & (bitwise AND)
                  1 if both are 1. Otherwise, 0.
                

                

                   | (bitwise OR)
                  1 if any of them is 1. Otherwise, 0.
                

                

                   ^ (bitwise XOR)
                  1 if both are different.
                

                

                   << (Bitwise shift left)
                  Shift bits to left side. Multiply by 2 on each shift.
                

                

                   >> (Bitwise shift right)
                  Shift bits to right side. Divide by 2 on each shift.
